# INTERCOMPANY SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Intercompany Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into as of [DATE], by and between:

1. **Printspace Studios Ltd**, a company incorporated and registered in the United Kingdom, having its registered office at 74 Kingsland Road, London, E2 8DL, UK ("Service Provider"); and

2. **theprintspace inc**, a company incorporated and registered in the United States, having its principal place of business at [ADDRESS], USA ("Recipient").

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this Agreement is to document and formalize the intercompany services provided by Printspace Studios Ltd to theprintspace inc and to establish the cost allocation and reimbursement mechanism in compliance with applicable tax laws, including IRS transfer pricing regulations.

## 2. Scope of Services
Printspace Studios Ltd shall provide management and administrative services related to the setup of theprintspace inc, specifically focusing on the directors' time spent on the setup process, including but not limited to:

* Executive and director-level oversight
* Initial financial and administrative structuring
* HR and payroll setup
* IT and software implementation

## 3. Cost Allocation & Pricing
The costs incurred by Printspace Studios Ltd for services rendered to theprintspace inc shall be recharged based on a fair allocation of actual costs, including:

* Employee remuneration for time spent on US operations
* Employer National Insurance Contributions (NIC)
* Employer pension contributions

The pricing methodology is based on the **percentage of time spent by directors** on the US operations, ensuring that only the proportionate costs are allocated.

## 4. Payment Terms

* This is a **one-off charge** and the invoice was issued on **30/06/2024**.
* Payment shall be made within **30 days** of receipt of invoice.
* Payments shall be settled via the **intercompany account** between Printspace Studios Ltd and theprintspace inc.

## 5. Compliance with Transfer Pricing Regulations
Both parties acknowledge that intercompany transactions must be conducted at arm's length and in accordance with applicable transfer pricing laws, including:

* UK and US tax regulations (IRS Section 482 and OECD Transfer Pricing Guidelines)
* Maintenance of proper documentation to support the reasonableness of the charges
* Filing of IRS Form 5472 (as applicable) to disclose intercompany transactions

## 6. Term and Termination

* This Agreement shall remain in effect until the completion of payment related to the one-off charge.
* Termination shall not affect any outstanding payment obligations accrued before termination.

## 7. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the United Kingdom and the United States.

## 8. Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements related to the subject matter.
