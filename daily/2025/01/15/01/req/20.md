## 2.
Я хочу ответить клиенту так:
~~~
1) Stripe Connect is used for marketplaces: intermediary platforms that accept payments from some individuals (buyers) for the goods and services of other individuals (sellers), and then transfer the received payment to those same sellers, minus their commission.
2) The developers of Shopify and BigCommerce do not aim to provide their clients with the possibility of developing such intermediary platforms (marketplaces).
At the same time, the developers of Shopify and BigCommerce only work under the SaaS model and do not provide the source code of Shopify and BigCommerce, so Shopify and BigCommerce are unlikely to be the best choice for setting up an intermediary platform (marketplace).
3) The developers of Shopify and BigCommerce do not offer a ready-made integration with Stripe Connect.
4) To integrate Shopify with Stripe Connect, you need to develop a «Payment extension»: https://shopify.dev/docs/apps/build/payments
5) To integrate BigCommerce with Stripe Connect, you need to use the «Payments API»: https://developer.bigcommerce.com/docs/store-operations/payments
6) Shopify is interested in its own payment system — Shopify Payments.
Since integration with Stripe Connect will work around Shopify Payments, Shopify will charge you an additional transaction fee (it could be +2%, +1%, or +0.5% – depending on the plan).
Overall, as I have already said in item 2, Shopify is not the best solution for implementing the business model you need.
7)
7.1) BigCommerce is an outsider compared to Shopify.
7.2) BigCommerce entered the market significantly later than Shopify and was originally developed with one idea: to become a Shopify clone.
7.3) Therefore, they have to work with lower margins and, in particular, they still have to refrain from introducing a transaction fee.
7.4) But BigCommerce has its own trick: if your turnover is above $400K per year, you will have to switch to the highest pricing plan «Enterprise» with unpublished rates: essentially, BigCommerce salespeople will extort from you as much as they can get.
With your intermediary business model (with, obviously, low margins), this is especially dangerous: your partner-sellers will be generating turnover for you, and you will have to pay BigCommerce.
7.5) Also, as I have already said in item 2, the SaaS architecture and closed source code do not particularly favor your business model.
~~~
